ABOUT US





OUR MOTIVE

     

        Our primary objective is to unite talented sculptors who are currently seeking employment,
        offering them a platform to showcase their exceptional creations and, in turn, 
        facilitating opportunities for meaningful artistic employment.

OUR ORGANISATION AND TEAM

MOORTOKAAR.COM, our organization, is dedicated to empowering unemployed sculptors by providing them with meaningful employment opportunities. We are the developers behind MOORTIKAAR.COM, working tirelessly to enhance and expand this platform.
